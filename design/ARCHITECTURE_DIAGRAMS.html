  T --> DS["DataScopeAdapter per-row 行级裁剪"]:::core
  DS --> D
  K --> D

  D -->|够答即停| ANS["生成回答, 每字段可溯源 (不编造)"]:::core
  ANS --> DONE["SSE: tool_call/result(call_id配对) → artifact → answer → done"]:::bau

  D -.检测到变更请求.-> W{"显式 write mode?"}:::core
  W -->|否| NUDGE["提示: 请切换写入模式"]:::bau
  W -->|是, effect=has_side_effect| PROP["生成 WriteProposal 持久化"]:::bau
  PROP --> CONFIRM["用户确认 (人工)"]:::core
  CONFIRM --> EXEC["executor 复核 权限/版本/expiry/幂等 → 执行"]:::core
  EXEC --> DONE
